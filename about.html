About WIE

IEEE Women In Engineering is the largest international professional organization dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering. India viewed as a potential powerhouse of innovations, is no exception. The fact, that, institutes of scientific learning are open to all the citizens of a country, but potential females still do not come up and hesitate at the thresholds of laboratories. IEEE has, thus, taken a step forth to help promote all the women across the globe with IEEE Women In Engineering.
